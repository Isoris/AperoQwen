Resumé

I am a Molecular biologist. Currently, I am looking for a PhD position in the field of aquaculture. I would like to make my own research contribution towards aquaculture genomics. Now, I live in Thailand and about to begin my master thesis. My research project’s objective is to develop a Proof of Concept of an in vitro transcribed mRNA vaccine against Carp Edema Virus Disease using a low-cost experimental design (June 2021 - May 2022)
